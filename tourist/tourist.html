Bontang Kuala

The very large area of Eastern Indonesia has indeed encouraged local residents to be familiar with the sea. No exception in Bontang, East Kalimantan. There is a Bontang Kuala village where almost all of its buildings are built on the water! Unique, right? This location is the point to change to Beras Basah Island and Segajah Island. Therefore, leaving for leaving early in the morning so that you still have time to tour Bontang Kuala. Visiting Bontang Kuala is not complete without tasting the local specialties, which are mostly seafood. To be sure, the fish is very fresh because it is harvested directly from the residents' ponds right before cooking. No wonder, one of Bontang's specialties, Gammi Bawis, once represented East Kalimantan in the nomination for the 2017 Anugerah Pesona Indonesia Most Popular Food Category.
