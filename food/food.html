Gammi

Don't call yourself from Bontang if you haven't try Sambal Gammi, Sambal Gammi is a local food that Bontang people love to eat. We eat Sambal Gammi on a hot plate, and sometimes it's mixed with Bawis, Chicken, Squid, Shrimp, or even Egg.
